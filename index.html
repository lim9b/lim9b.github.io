We Are Dream-Makers.

Somos Criadores de Sonhos.

We created lim9 because we wanted to develop something Diferent, something that went beyond the traditional Creative Studio. We believe that quality comes from a "Less Is More" attitude and therefore we don't work with ASSIGNMENTS (momentary work dictated by a budget and rushed by profits), we only work with DREAMS, may them be humble or sumptuous!

Criámos a lim9 porque queríamos desenvolver algo Diferente, algo que fosse Além do Tradicional Estúdio Criativo. Acreditamos que a Qualidade advém de uma atitude "Less Is More" e por isso não trabalhamos com PROJECTOS (trabalhos pontuais ditados por um orçamento e apressados pelo lucro), só trabalhamos com SONHOS, sejam eles humildes ou suntuosos!

Nothing is too small or too big for us. May your Dream be opening a restaurant, achieving 1 million clients or being the next Apple, we can guarantee our full commitment and dedication to bring Your Vision to life. Thus we work With You during the necessary timespan, using Creativity, Technology and Marketing to create Innovation and Make Dreams Come True.

Nada é pequeno ou grande demais para nós. Seja o seu Sonho abrir um restaurante, conquistar 1 milhão de clientes ou ser a próxima Apple, garantimos sempre todo o nosso empenho e dedicação para dar vida à sua Visão. Assim trabalhamos CONSIGO durante o tempo necessário, usando Criatividade, Tecnologia e Marketing para criar Inovação e Concretizar Sonhos.

This is why we work with Dream Budgets defined By You for any given timespan. It's according to this Budget that we'll begin the Creative Production making use of our International Team's versatility so that nothing is ever beyond Lim9's reach. The word "Impossible" simply Doesn't Exist on our Dictionary. There's nothing we can't do but that doesn't mean we'll do anything.

Por isso trabalhamos com Orçamentos de Sonho que são definidos Por Si para um determinado período de tempo. É de acordo com este orçamento que iniciaremos a Produção Criativa fazendo uso da versatilidade da nossa Equipe Internacional para que nada esteja fora do alcance da Lim9. A palavra "Impossível" simplesmente Não Consta do nosso Dicionário.

Os Seus Sonhos

Design

We believe in designing with an Intent, with a Purpose, a Reason.

For us Design is the Backbone of every project and the Secret Weapon to Inovation. It's our belief that a Great Design is essential to any quality product and that is exactly Lim9's specialty: creating High Quality Products that Merge Technology and Design in a perfectly Natural Way.
Audio

To the exception of Smell, Hearing is the Only Sense capable of Traveling to the Subconscious and Rescuing Memories

Hearing is part of Everything we Live and it's so important that it allows us to feel things we didn't even remember we had ever felt. Either in the form of Music or Special Effects, Sound is a Magical Way to Associate a Feeling to Your Brand.
Development

Imagine you could comunicate with our Planet, what would you do?

That's exactly what Development is: the ability to comunicate with all technology and use it on our behalf to make impossible thing possible. Automating your World, creating Interactive Entertainment, turning Reality into a Game, with Technology your wish is our command.
Vídeo

If a Picture's worth One Thousand words, how much are 24 Frames per Second?

The creation of video forever changed our world with the Movie Magic. By merging images and sound, video became without a doubt the prefered medium for content consumption nowadays and in a world where you have just 20 seconds to get a client's attention it's the perfect way to make the difference."
Marketing

Marketing é a arte de criar e manter o seu próprio mercado.

Muito mais do que publicidade ou comunicação o Marketing é a sua Língua Própria, a forma como contata com todos os seus clientes e como introduz novos utilizadores à sua marca. É a sua oportunidade de fazer a sua mensagem chegar a milhões de pessoas por isso essa mensagem tem de ser Perfeita.
